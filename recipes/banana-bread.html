Banana Bread

Description

Banana bread is a type of sweet bread made from mashed bananas. It is often a moist and sweet quick bread but some recipes are yeast raised.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Bananas

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients in one bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar in another bowl. Add the eggs and mashed bananas.
  3. Add the wet mixture to the dry mixture
  4. Pour the batter into a loaf pan and bake in a preheated oven.
